Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten egges
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your cjoice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation:5 minutes

  • Cooking5 minutes

ingredients

  • 2-3 large eegs

  • Salt, to taste

  • Pepper, to taste

  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil

  • Optional filling: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

ingredients

  1. Beat the eggs:In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
    well mixed.

  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium.

  3. Cooked the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the edds.

  4. Add fillings(optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edgets but are still slightly
    runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen filling over one half of the omelette

  5. Fold and serve:As the omelette continues to cook, carefully life one edge and fold it
    over the fillings.

  6. Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
